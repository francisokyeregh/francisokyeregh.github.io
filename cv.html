Professional Profile

I am a Ph.D. student in Statistics at Florida State University. My research focuses on high-dimensional statistics, statistical learning, penalized regression, variable selection, bootstrap stability selection, and interpretable machine learning. I am interested in developing statistically rigorous methods for predictive modeling with applications in healthcare, education, sports analytics, and public policy.
